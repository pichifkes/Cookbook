### 2. קינוח "סירואלה" (Siruela) - מתכון באנגלית

*   **Estimated Preparation Time:** 5.5 hours (including baking and cooling)
*   **Estimated Difficulty:** Hard

*(Note: This recipe was sent by email and is written in English, as per the original source.)*

#### **Ingredients**

**For the cream:**
*   500g cream 33%
*   130g butter
*   300g sugar
*   10 egg yolks

**For the meringue:**
*   400g egg white
*   200g sugar
*   20g lemon juice
*   200g powdered sugar
*   20g starch
*   200g almond petals

#### **Instructions**

1.  **To prepare the cream:** First, you need to mix cream, butter, and sugar until smooth, then add the yolks. Bring to a boil on a water bath and cook, stirring constantly for one hour. Cool well.
2.  **To prepare the meringues:** Mix the egg white with the sugar to form a stiff froth. Then add the remaining ingredients: lemon juice, powdered sugar, and starch and continue to beat. Finally, add almond petals and mix with hands.
3.  Bake the meringue in the oven at a temperature of 100°C for 4 hours. If your oven has an air circulation function, it is good. If not, you need to provide a little access to air by slightly opening the oven door. The meringue should be firm.
4.  **Assembly:** Cut the resulting meringue cake in half. Coat both parts with the cream. Add prunes and walnuts between the layers and connect them.
5.  Decorate the dessert with toasted almonds. Ready!

#### **מצרכים (סיכום חלקי)**

4.  **הרכבה:**
    *   על שכבת בצק אפויה אחת מורחים ריבת פובידל.
    *   מעל הריבה מורחים את הקצף (מרנג) ואופים אותו לייבוש בחום נמוך.
    *   על שכבת הבצק השנייה מורחים את שני סוגי הקרם בשכבות.
    *   מניחים את שכבת הבצק עם המרנג האפוי מעל שכבת הקרם.
5.  מקררים היטב לפני ההגשה.
